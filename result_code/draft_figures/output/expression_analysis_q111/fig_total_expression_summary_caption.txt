METHODOLOGY:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Total mRNA per cell:
  Total = N_spots + (I_cluster_total / I_single_peak) / N_nuclei

Peak intensity normalization:
  - Slide-specific I_single_peak determined via KDE
  - Scott's bandwidth method for KDE
  - Minimum 50 spots required for peak estimation

This metric combines:
  1. Single mRNA: Individual diffraction-limited spots
  2. Clustered mRNA: Aggregated signal normalized by peak intensity

INTERPRETATION:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Total mRNA per cell represents the most comprehensive quantification of transcript
abundance, capturing both dispersed single molecules and high-density aggregates.
Regional differences may reflect:
  - Intrinsic transcriptional differences
  - Cell-type composition variations
  - Disease-specific vulnerability patterns

Inter-mouse variability may be driven by:
  - Age differences
  - Treatment conditions (aCSF, NTC, UNT)
  - Biological heterogeneity within genotype
